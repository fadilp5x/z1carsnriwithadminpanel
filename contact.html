Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for an NRI to rent a car?

We require a copy of driving license or other valid documents.

Can I book a car from the UAE before I travel to Kerala?

Absolutely. Most of our clients book in advance from the World. You can finalize everything over WhatsApp or phone, and the car will be ready for you upon arrival.

Do your rental prices include fuel?

Our standard rental packages do not include fuel. The vehicle will be provided with a full tank, and we request that it be returned with a full tank.
